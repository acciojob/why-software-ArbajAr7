Why I want to become a developer?

people are often drawn to becoming developers due to their passion for technology, enjoyment of creative problem-solving, desire for job opportunities and career growth, flexibility in work arrangements, high earning potential, the chance to make a meaningful impact through software, and the opportunity for continuous learning and intellectual challenge. These motivations combine to attract individuals to the field of software development, where they can fulfill their interests and goals while contributing to the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
